Institute of Clinical Surgery

  • About Institute
  • Leadership

The institute is focused on the development and introduction of new methods of surgery into clinical practice.

The institute includes the following divisions:

  • Laboratory of Woman, Mother and Child Health
  • Laboratory of Clinical and Experimental Pediatric Otorhinolaryngology
  • Laboratory of Surgical Gastroenterology and Endoscopy
  • Department of Pediatric Reconstructive and Plastic Surgery
  • Pediatric Surgery Department
  • Department of Abdominal Oncology
  • Department of Experimental Surgery
  • Department of Anesthesiology and Resuscitation
  • Department of Basic and Applied Research in Surgery
