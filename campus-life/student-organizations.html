Student Organizations and Activities

Pirogov Medical University offers wide opportunities for creativity, sports and self-improvement. In addition to their academic and scientific research, students can choose their own extracurricular adventures, from exciting sets of ways to participate in music, dance, theater and sports to a dozen groups of volunteers who participate in organizing the blood donation movement, patronize boarding schools for sick children, participate in actions of charitable foundations, help in holding university events...

With our student societies and sports clubs, anyone has countless opportunities to make your free time the way he want.

JOIN US

Student Self-Government

Student Council

Coordination of different areas of student activities, interaction of all areas with each other, the organization of leisure time of students and support for new initiatives are the mission of the University Student Council. The Student Council is the representative collegial organization of student self-government. The composition of the Student Council includes representatives of all student organizations and prefects of faculties and dormitory.
