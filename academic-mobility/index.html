CURRENT EXCANGE PROGRAMMES

  • CURRENT EXCANGE PROGRAMMES

How to get involved

To participate in academic exchange programmes, we invite students of 2-6 courses that meet the following requirements:

  • students with average score - not lower than 4.0;
  • students who actively participate in the public and academic activities of the University;
  • students who speak a foreign language (English or the language of the country of academic exchange program at a level sufficient for internships and for communication in everyday life);
  • students who have the opportunity during the period July-August to provide assistance in accompanying, if necessary, foreign students arriving from foreign universities for academic exchange programs in the University (meeting/transfer to the airport, assistance in accommodation in the dormitory of Pirogov Russian National Research Medical University, support to the practice base, assistance in carrying out the cultural program).

For some exchange programmes (CIS countries), knowledge of a foreign language is not required. For some exchange programmes, applicants should pass an English proficiency test.

Algorithm for testing foreign language skills for participation in the international exchange programmes

The academic mobility group informs students about the date, time and location of testing for international exchange programmes by e-mail or telephone and by placing information on the academic mobility group page on the official University website.

Students invited for testing should come at the appointed time to the specified classroom. During testing in the classroom, students sit one by one at separate tables. Students complete a written assignment on paper (answers to the test and essay) and undergo computer testing. Computer testing is performed in several stages (recording, listening). Students will receive test results via email. The results will be also published on the academic mobility page on the official website of the University.

Students wishing to participate in exchange programmes must submit an application to the academic mobility group of the international faculty (1, Ostrovityanova str., 4th floor, office 416, t. 8-495-434 - 30-74, e-mail: interdep@rsmu.ru) from February 1 to March 1.
 

Application documents

If you want to apply you need to provide next documents:

  • Application form with the indication in priority of three universities where you would like to undergo academic exchange program
  • Motivation letter in English (for some programs - in Russian), in which it is necessary to indicate: justification of the reasons for choosing this particular university/program; goals, objectives, expected results (educational, professional, social); justification of the advantages of his candidacy for participation in the international exchange program
  • Approved application from the dean of the faculty (the letter is written by hand and certified by the dean of the faculty)
  • Сopy of the general passport (first page and registration, if applicable)
  • Copy of the international passport (first page)
  • Photo 3x4 cm., pasted in the application form
  • Copies of diplomas, certificates (scientific achievements, participation in competitions, awards, letters of acknowledgment)
  • Personal data processing consent
  • Consent for the cross-border transfer of student personal data
  • Confirmation of accepting the conditions for exchange programmes participation
  • Аpplication Form
  • Motivation Letter
  • Personal Data Processing Consent
  • Approved Application from the Dean of the Faculty
  • Confirmation of Accepting the Conditions for Exchange Programs Participation
  • Consent for the cross-border transfer of student personal data
Actions after winning a contest selection

Students who have successfully passed the competitive selection must submit additional documents no later than the date specified for each program separately:

  • Vaccination certificate
  • Student safety induction
  • Obligation of medical insurance (form attached) + issued medical insurance for the entire period of practical training (copy)
  • Approved application addressed to the rector, written by hand

Upon successful completion of the competitive selection and receiving an invitation from a foreign partner university, the student independently collects and submits to the consulate a package of documents for obtaining a visa.

  • Vaccination certificate
  • Application form addressed to the rector (Rus)
  • Student safety induction
  • Health insurance obligation
Reporting documentation

One month after arriving at a partner university, students must send a study report to the group of academic mobility of an international teacher at interdep@rsmu.ru.

Not later than within 30 days after trip, the students - members of program must provide a report on the passage of internship to the group of academic mobility of the international faculty.
